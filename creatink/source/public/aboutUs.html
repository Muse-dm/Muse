Muse - Origins

For instrumentalists and music enthusiasts alike, we present a 2-hour interactive workshop on Chord Theory and Compositional Arrangements.

What gives a melody line perspective? How does background music and Harmony affect the mood of the song in general? If you've ever wondered about this or tinkered around with composing in general, here's a look at how instruments and harmonies build an entire song.

We promise a fun, interactive evening, with some songs and ideas to take back home, and hopefully a new perspective on the beautiful mystery that is music. Age Group: Age is no bar, the teaching will be very visual, aural and easy to grasp.

About Us

MUSE is a group of young event organizers and artists. We offer experiences like treks, specialized trips, treasure hunts, house concerts, unique game nights, tournaments, house parties and customized events. We even offer collaborative support for other artists, who may need certain talents for their projects, building a network that enables more creative ideas to come to fruition.
